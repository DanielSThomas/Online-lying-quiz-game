Fib Trivia

The goal of the game is to earn points by creating fake answers to questions, in order to trick other players, while also guessing the correct answer.

Each round, all players will get the same question. Players should come up with a fake, but believable, answer to the question.

(The goal is not to guess the correct answer yet)

Next all fake answers will be mixed in with the correct answer.

Players who guess the correct answer gain points.

However, players will also gain points if another player guesses their fake answer.

The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.
